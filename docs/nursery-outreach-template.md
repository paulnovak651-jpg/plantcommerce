# Nursery Consent Outreach Template

Use this template when contacting nurseries about inclusion on PlantCommerce. Personalize the bracketed fields before sending.

**Subject:** PlantCommerce — Including [Nursery Name] in Our Plant Comparison Platform

---

Hi [First Name / "there"],

My name is Paul, and I run Even Flow Nursery LLC. I'm building a platform called **PlantCommerce** that helps permaculture growers compare plant availability and pricing across specialty nurseries.

I'm reaching out because we'd love to include **[Nursery Name]** on the platform, and I want to make sure you're comfortable with that before we do.

### What PlantCommerce does

PlantCommerce aggregates publicly listed product information — plant names, prices, and availability — from nursery websites and presents it in a searchable comparison format. Think of it as a plant-focused shopping comparison tool for the permaculture and edible-landscaping community.

### How your nursery would appear

- Your nursery name, location, and a link to your website displayed on every listing
- Product names, prices, and availability shown as listed on your site
- Every product links directly back to your product page — we drive traffic to you, not away from you

### What we DON'T do

- **We don't sell plants.** PlantCommerce is a comparison tool, not a storefront
- **We don't undercut pricing.** We display your prices as-is to help growers find what they need
- **We don't scrape personal data.** We only pull publicly listed product catalog information
- **We don't store or redistribute your product images**

### Opting out

Your participation is entirely voluntary. If you'd prefer not to be included — now or at any point in the future — just reply to this email and we'll remove all of your data from the platform within 48 hours.

If I don't hear back, I'll follow up once. If there's still no response, we won't include your nursery until we have your explicit approval.

Thanks for your time, and thanks for growing great plants.

Paul
